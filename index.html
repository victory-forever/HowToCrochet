Crochet Books

First of all I highly recommend the book “Corner To Corner Crochet” by Jess Coppom. It`s my favorite book to learn how to c2c crochet. It contains full comprehensive information, everything you need to know about c2c crochet. The information is perceived very easily and understandable. And the book also contains 15 beautiful contemporary C2C projects (blankets, pillows, rug, different clothes, etc.) that use the basic technique to create a stunning effect.

You will learn:

  • Crochet Basics

  • C2C Basics:

    • How to Increase

    • How to Decrease

    • Changing Color

    • Managing Yarns

    • How To Weave In Ends

    • Finishing Off A Raw Edge

    • How to make borders

    • Joining C2C Pieces

  • Making A Tassel

  • Adding Fringe

  • Tools and Materials

  • Us And Uk Terminology

  • Abbreviations

  • Lots Of Useful Tips

I just love this book!! If you are interested you can find this wonderful book here: “Corner To Corner Crochet” by Jess Coppom
